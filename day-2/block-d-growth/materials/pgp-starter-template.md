# PGP Starter Template — Example Growth Areas & Sentence Starters

Use these as a jumping-off point. Pick the growth areas that resonate with your case context, adapt them, and make them specific enough that you could check in next month and know whether progress happened.

---

## Example Growth Areas

Pick 2-3 that fit your situation. Then rewrite them in your own words with specific context.

| Growth Area | What This Might Sound Like in Your PGP |
|---|---|
| **Stakeholder communication** | "I need to improve how I translate technical work into business impact for non-technical stakeholders." |
| **Prioritization and saying no** | "I tend to say yes to every request. I need to use RICE or a similar framework to make explicit trade-offs." |
| **Delegation and letting go** | "I default to doing the work myself instead of coaching my team to do it. This limits our throughput." |
| **Executive presence** | "I lose credibility in exec meetings because I lead with methodology instead of conclusions (BLUF)." |
| **Technical depth** | "I need to deepen my understanding of [specific area: dbt, experimentation design, ML ops] to earn my team's trust on technical decisions." |
| **Hiring and team building** | "I have never run a structured hiring loop. I need to build this muscle before my next open req." |
| **Feedback and difficult conversations** | "I avoid giving constructive feedback until it becomes a performance issue. I need to practice SBI regularly." |
| **Data infrastructure literacy** | "I don't understand our data stack well enough to make informed build-vs-buy decisions." |

## Sentence Starters for Development Actions

Use these to turn vague intentions into concrete, time-bound actions:

- "By [date], I will have [specific deliverable or behavior change]..."
- "Starting [next week / next month], I will [new habit or practice]..."
- "I will ask [specific person] to [provide feedback / mentor me / shadow me] on [skill] by [date]..."
- "I will complete [course / book / training] on [topic] by [date] and apply it by [doing X]..."
- "I will practice [skill] by [specific method: presenting at team meeting, writing a memo weekly, etc.] at least [frequency] for [duration]..."

## Example: A Complete Growth Area + Action

**Growth Area:** "I struggle to present technical findings to non-technical stakeholders in a way that drives decisions."

**Development Actions:**
1. Read the BLUF and executive writing slides from Block C and apply the structure to my next 3 stakeholder updates (by end of month 1).
2. Practice a 2-minute elevator pitch of my roadmap with my manager and ask for specific feedback on clarity (month 1, week 2).
3. Volunteer to present at the next product review meeting — prepare using the executive narrative template and get feedback from a peer beforehand (by end of month 2).
4. Ask the VP of Product to rate the clarity of my next quarterly update on a 1-4 scale and share what was unclear (month 3).

**Support Needed:** Time on my manager's calendar for pitch practice. Honest feedback from a peer who is strong at exec communication.

*Adapt these to your case context. The starter examples above are scaffolding — your final PGP should reflect YOUR growth areas, not these generic ones.*
